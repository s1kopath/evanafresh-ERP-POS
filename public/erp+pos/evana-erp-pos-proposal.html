Solution Overview

A single connected platform — the POS and the ERP share the same live database, so a sale at the till instantly updates stock, customer balances and the financial books.

🛒 POS & Sales

Barcode, non-barcode & weight-based selling. Cash, card, partial & credit. Returns, refunds, held bills. ZATCA e-invoice on every sale. Works offline.

📦 Inventory & Buying

Real-time stock, min levels, low-stock & expiry alerts, valuation, auto-reorder, purchase entry/returns, price history.

💰 ERP Finance

Customer/supplier ledgers, expenses, payroll, cash & bank, AR/AP, P&L, balance sheet — multi-branch & consolidated.

Architecture & access

DeploymentCloud-hosted (SaaS) with secure, encrypted daily backups. Data residency in Saudi Arabia.
Offline capabilityPOS continues selling with no internet; transactions queue locally and auto-sync on reconnect.
DevicesWeb browser (desktop POS & back-office), Android/iOS owner & reporting app, standard POS hardware.
Multi-branchCentral HQ view, per-branch operation, inter-branch stock transfers, consolidated reporting.
SecurityRole-based access (owner, manager, accountant, cashier); full audit trail.
ComplianceZATCA Phase-2 (FATOORA) e-invoicing — QR-coded simplified invoices & XML reporting; 15% VAT handling.

Standard POS hardware supported

  • Barcode scanners (1D/2D)
  • Electronic weighing scales (label & direct)
  • Thermal receipt printers
  • Cash drawers
  • Customer-facing displays
  • Label / barcode printers

Why QTEC for Evana Fresh

Grocery-specific workflows (weight items, expiry & near-expiry promotions, fast/slow/dead-stock), built-in ZATCA compliance, offline-first tills, and a platform that scales cleanly from 2 stores to many — without re-implementation.
